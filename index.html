King Size Pizza's

Italian pizzas are known for their thin, crispy crust and fresh, high-quality ingredients.
Each bite offers a perfect balance of tangy tomato sauce,
creamy mozzarella, and flavorful toppings.

Margherita

Pizza Margherita is a classic Neapolitan pizza made with fresh tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. It was created in honor of Queen Margherita of Savoy and represents the colors of the Italian flag. Simple yet flavorful

Quattro Stagioni

Pizza Quattro Stagioni means "Four Seasons" and is divided into four sections, each with different toppings. Common ingredients include artichokes (spring), mushrooms (autumn), ham (winter), and olives or tomatoes (summer).

Prosciutto e Funghi

Pizza Prosciutto e Funghi is a classic Italian pizza topped with cooked ham (prosciutto cotto) and mushrooms. It combines the savory taste of ham with the earthy flavor of mushrooms over a tomato and mozzarella base.

One special and iconic food in Italy is Lasagna alla Bolognese 🇮🇹. It’s a rich, oven-baked pasta dish made with layers of fresh pasta sheets, slow-cooked meat ragù (Bolognese sauce), béchamel (white sauce), and Parmesan cheese. Originating from Emilia-Romagna, especially Bologna, lasagna is a comforting and flavorful dish that showcases Italy’s love for homemade pasta and hearty sauces.
